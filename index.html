Welcome To My Official Anime List


#1

The first anime on the list is Hunter X Hunter. This is a show about an orphan boy who meets up with 3 other people all wanting the same goal; To become Hunters. They go on the adventure of a lifetime. Traveling the world and even hopping into a video game. Solid story, Lovable characters, and Fun humor.


Rating: 9/10

Where To Watch: Hulu, Netflix, Gogoanime
